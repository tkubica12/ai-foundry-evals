## 1. Company Overview

TechVibe Solutions is a technology company founded in 2018 and headquartered in Austin, Texas. We employ approximately 850 people across engineering, product, design, sales, and operations. Our mission is **making technology accessible and delightful** for businesses and consumers alike.

We build cloud-based productivity tools, developer platforms, and data analytics solutions that serve over 3,000 enterprise customers worldwide. Our core values are transparency, craftsmanship, empathy, and continuous learning.

This handbook outlines the policies, benefits, and expectations that apply to all full-time employees of TechVibe Solutions. Contractors and part-time staff should refer to their individual agreements for applicable terms.

## 2. PTO & Time Off Policy

### Paid Time Off (PTO)

- All full-time employees receive **20 days of PTO per year**.
- PTO is accrued monthly at a rate of **1.67 days per month**, beginning on your start date.
- PTO may be used for vacation, personal days, or any other personal time off needs.

### Sick Leave

- Employees receive **5 sick days per year**, granted in full on January 1 each year.
- Sick days may be used for your own illness or to care for an immediate family member (spouse, child, or parent).
- Sick days do not roll over and are forfeited if unused at year-end.

### Carryover & Forfeiture

- Unused PTO **rolls over up to a maximum of 5 days** into the following calendar year.
- Any PTO balance exceeding 5 days is **forfeited on December 31**.
- Sick days are not eligible for carryover or payout.

### Requesting Time Off

- All PTO requests must be submitted through the TimeAway portal and approved by your direct manager.
- Requests of **3 or more consecutive days** must be submitted at least **2 weeks in advance**.
- Requests of 1–2 days require at least 3 business days' notice, except in emergencies.
- Managers may deny requests based on business needs, project deadlines, or team coverage.

### Company Holidays

TechVibe observes 10 paid company holidays per year, including New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving (Thursday and Friday), Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, and two floating holidays chosen by the employee.

## 3. Remote Work Policy

### Hybrid Work Model

TechVibe operates under a **hybrid work model**. All employees with in-office-eligible roles are required to work from the office a **minimum of 2 days per week**. The designated in-office days are **Tuesday and Thursday**. Managers may request additional in-office days based on team needs.

### Fully Remote Positions

Certain roles may be designated as fully remote. Fully remote arrangements require **written approval from a Vice President (VP)** and must be reviewed annually. Fully remote employees are expected to travel to the Austin headquarters at least once per quarter for team meetings and planning sessions.

### Home Office Stipend

Employees in approved remote or hybrid roles are eligible for a **$500 annual home office stipend** to cover equipment such as monitors, keyboards, desk chairs, and internet upgrades. Receipts must be submitted through the ExpenseTrack portal for reimbursement.

### Core Hours & Availability

All employees, regardless of location, must be available during **core hours of 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM Central Time (CT)**. Meetings, stand-ups, and collaborative sessions are scheduled during this window. Outside of core hours, employees have flexibility to structure their workday as they see fit, provided they fulfill their role responsibilities and maintain a minimum 40-hour work week.

### Workspace Requirements

Remote workers must maintain a dedicated, secure workspace with a reliable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps download). Video-capable setups are required for all remote participants in meetings.

## 4. Health & Benefits

### Health Insurance Plans

TechVibe offers two health insurance plans for full-time employees:

| Plan | Type | Monthly Employee Cost | Dental | Vision |
|------|------|-----------------------|--------|--------|
| **TechVibe Premium** | PPO | $150/month | ✅ Included | ✅ Included |
| **TechVibe Basic** | HMO | $75/month | ❌ Not included | ❌ Not included |

- Enrollment is available during annual open enrollment (November 1–15) or within 30 days of a qualifying life event.
- Dependents (spouse/domestic partner and children under 26) may be added to either plan at additional cost.
- The **TechVibe Premium** plan provides broader provider network access, lower deductibles, and includes comprehensive dental and vision coverage. The **TechVibe Basic** plan does not cover dental or vision services.

### Retirement — 401(k)

- TechVibe provides a 401(k) retirement plan through Fidelity Investments.
- The company matches **50% of employee contributions up to 6% of salary**.
  - Example: If you earn $100,000 and contribute 6% ($6,000), TechVibe contributes $3,000.
- Company match is subject to a **3-year vesting schedule**: 33% vested after Year 1, 66% after Year 2, and 100% after Year 3.
- Employees are eligible to enroll on their first day of employment.

### Professional Development

- Each employee receives a **$2,000 per year professional development budget**.
- Eligible expenses include conferences, online courses, certifications, books, and workshops.
- Pre-approval from your manager is required before incurring the expense.
- Unused professional development funds do not carry over to the next year.

### Additional Benefits

- **Life Insurance:** Company-paid basic life insurance at 1x annual salary.
- **Employee Assistance Program (EAP):** Free confidential counseling and support services.
- **Commuter Benefits:** Pre-tax transit and parking deductions up to IRS limits.
- **Gym Membership Discount:** 30% discount at participating fitness centers.

## 5. IT Security Policies

### Device Security

- All company-issued and personal devices used for work must have **full-disk encryption** enabled (BitLocker for Windows, FileVault for macOS).
- Employees must connect through the **company VPN** when accessing internal systems from outside the office network.
- **No personal USB drives** may be connected to company laptops. Only company-approved external storage devices are permitted.
- Laptops must be locked (Win+L or Cmd+Ctrl+Q) whenever left unattended, even within the office.

### Password & Authentication

- All passwords must be a **minimum of 12 characters** and include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
- Passwords must be **rotated every 90 days**. The system will prompt you automatically.
- **Multi-factor authentication (MFA)** is required for all company systems, including email, VPN, Slack, and cloud platforms.
- Do not reuse passwords across company and personal accounts.

### Incident Reporting

- Any suspected security incident — including phishing attempts, unauthorized access, lost or stolen devices, or data breaches — must be **reported within 1 hour** of discovery.
- Report incidents by emailing **security@techvibe.com** or calling the Security Operations Center at (512) 555-0199.
- Do not attempt to investigate or remediate incidents on your own. The security team will coordinate the response.

### Software & Access

- Only IT-approved software may be installed on company devices. Submit requests through the IT Service Desk.
- Access to company systems is granted on a least-privilege basis and reviewed quarterly.
- Terminated employees' access is revoked within 2 hours of separation.

## 6. Expense Reimbursement

### Submission Process

- All business expenses must be submitted through the **ExpenseTrack portal** within **30 days** of the date the expense was incurred.
- Each submission must include an itemized receipt and a brief business justification.
- Expenses submitted after 30 days may be denied at the discretion of the Finance team.

### Approval Thresholds

| Expense Amount | Approval Required |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Up to $100 | No additional approval (auto-approved) |
| $100.01 – $1,000 | **Manager approval** |
| Over $1,000 | **VP approval** |

### Travel Expenses

- **Flights:** Economy class is the standard for all domestic and international travel. Business class may be approved by a VP for flights exceeding 6 hours.
- **Hotels:** The nightly hotel limit is **$200/night**. Exceptions for high-cost cities (San Francisco, New York, London) may be approved by a manager with documented justification.
- **Ground Transportation:** Rideshare, taxis, and rental cars are reimbursable with receipts. Rental cars must be mid-size or smaller unless traveling with 3+ colleagues.
- **Meals (non-client):** Up to $50/day for individual meal expenses during travel.

### Client Entertainment

- **Client meals** are reimbursable up to **$75 per person**.
- All client entertainment expenses must include the names of attendees and the business purpose.
- Alcohol is limited to a reasonable amount and must not exceed 30% of the total meal cost.

## 7. Performance Reviews

### Review Cycle

- TechVibe conducts **annual performance reviews in Q4**, specifically during the months of **October and November**.
- Mid-year check-ins occur in April but are informal and not tied to compensation decisions.

### Review Process

- Your direct manager conducts your formal review.
- The review incorporates **360-degree feedback** collected from **3 peers** selected by the employee and approved by the manager.
- Self-assessments are submitted by the employee at least 2 weeks before the scheduled review meeting.
- Review meetings are typically 60 minutes and include goal-setting for the following year.

### Rating Scale

All employees are rated on the following scale:

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Exceeds Expectations** | Consistently delivers above role requirements; significant impact on team/company goals. |
| **Meets Expectations** | Reliably fulfills all role responsibilities; solid contributor to team objectives. |
| **Needs Improvement** | Performance gaps exist that must be addressed; a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) may be initiated. |

### Compensation & Merit Increases

- Merit increases are awarded in January following the review cycle.
- **Exceeds Expectations:** Eligible for a merit increase of **up to 8%**.
- **Meets Expectations:** Eligible for a merit increase of **up to 4%**.
- **Needs Improvement:** Not eligible for a merit increase. A PIP will be created with specific, measurable goals and a 90-day timeline.
- Promotions and equity refreshes are considered separately and may occur at any time based on business need and manager recommendation.

## 8. Parental Leave

### Paid Parental Leave

- **Birthing parents:** **16 weeks** of fully paid parental leave.
- **Non-birthing parents** (including adoptive and foster parents): **8 weeks** of fully paid parental leave.
- Parental leave begins on the date of birth, adoption placement, or foster placement.

### Additional Unpaid Leave

- An additional **4 weeks of unpaid leave** is available to all parents following the exhaustion of paid leave.
- Unpaid leave must be requested at the time of the initial leave notification.

### Notification Requirements

- Employees must notify HR of their anticipated leave **at least 8 weeks** before the expected leave start date.
- Notification should be submitted in writing to hr@techvibe.com and include the expected start and end dates.
- In cases of emergency or unexpected early delivery, notify HR as soon as practicable.

### Gradual Return Program

- TechVibe offers a **Gradual Return** option for employees returning from parental leave.
- Under this program, returning parents may work **60% of their normal hours for 4 weeks** while receiving **full pay**.
- The Gradual Return must be requested at least 2 weeks before the planned return date and approved by your manager and HR.

### Benefits During Leave

- Health insurance and all other benefits continue during paid parental leave with no change in employee contribution.
- PTO does not accrue during unpaid leave periods.

## 9. Code of Conduct

TechVibe is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free workplace. All employees are expected to:

- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with professionalism and respect.
- Report any concerns about harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior to HR or through the anonymous Ethics Hotline at (512) 555-0177.
- Comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and company policies.
- Protect confidential company information and intellectual property.
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts to your manager or Legal.

Violations of the Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
